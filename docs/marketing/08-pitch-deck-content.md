### Slide 6: HOW IT WORKS

**From document to decision in minutes, not hours.**

1. **INGEST** — Documents flow in from email, uploads, or core system APIs.
2. **UNDERSTAND** — AI reads, classifies, and extracts — understanding context, not just text.
3. **ROUTE** — Claims triaged and assigned to the right adjuster automatically.
4. **CHECK** — Compliance verified against 50-state rules and carrier playbooks.
5. **ACT** — Adjusters work in a unified workspace with full claim context.

The entire pipeline is AI-native — not AI bolted on. Every step learns from the last. Playbooks encode your carrier-specific rules so the workspace gets smarter with every claim.
