The content strategy relies on email capture through gated whitepapers and reports. Required infrastructure:

## 4. Social Media Launch Playbook

### LinkedIn (Primary Channel)

LinkedIn is the #1 social channel for reaching insurance executives. Company page + CEO personal account work in tandem.

#### Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-3)

| Week | Company Page | CEO Personal Account |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Set up page: logo, banner, description, custom CTA | Post: "We're building something for claims teams. More soon." (tease) |
| 2 | First post: industry insight (no product mention) | Post: observation about AI in insurance + personal take |
| 3 | Second post: "The problem with claims AI today" (narrative) | Post: "Why I left [X] to build an AI Claims Workspace" (founding story) |

#### Launch Week

| Day | Company Page | CEO Personal Account |
|---|---|---|
| Launch day | Announcement: "Introducing Charterwell — The AI Claims Workspace" + link to manifesto blog | Personal story: "Today we launched Charterwell. Here's why insurance needs its own AI workspace." + link |
| Day +2 | Share the manifesto blog with a key stat as hook | Engagement: respond to every comment on launch post |
| Day +4 | "The Claims Floor is Broken" — excerpt from manifesto | Behind-the-scenes: "What we learned building AI for legal that applies to insurance" |

#### Ongoing Cadence (Post-Launch)

| Content Type | Frequency | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| **Thought leadership** (opinions, industry analysis) | 1-2x/week | "Point solutions are the new legacy." / "Why 88% want AI but 7% scale it." |
| **Educational** (how claims AI works, buyer guides) | 1x/week | "5 questions to ask your claims AI vendor." / "What FNOL automation actually looks like." |
| **Company updates** (milestones, hires, partnerships) | 1x/week | Design partner announcements, team hires, conference attendance |
| **Engagement** (comment on industry posts, join discussions) | Daily | Comment on VP Claims, CIO, and analyst posts. Add value, don't pitch. |

#### LinkedIn Content Rules

1. **Lead with a strong opening line.** LinkedIn shows ~3 lines before "see more." The first sentence must hook.
2. **Use the language system.** Workspace, not platform. Claims professionals, not users. AI-native, not AI-powered.
3. **Include a number or specific outcome.** "15-20 minutes per FNOL, reduced to under 3" beats "dramatically faster."
4. **No hashtag spam.** Maximum 3 relevant hashtags: #insurtech #claimsmanagement #insuranceAI
5. **Tag sparingly.** Only tag people/companies when genuinely relevant. Never mass-tag for reach.
6. **Engage before promoting.** Build relationships in comments before expecting engagement on our posts.

### Twitter/X (Secondary Channel)

Lower priority. Use for:
- Conference live-commentary (ITC Vegas, CLM, IASA)
- Industry news commentary
- Amplifying LinkedIn content
- Engaging with insurance tech journalists and analysts

**Cadence:** 3-5 tweets/week. No dedicated content creation — repurpose LinkedIn content and add real-time commentary.

### YouTube (Tertiary Channel)

Build over time. Priority content:
- Product demo videos (when product is ready to show)
- Conference talk recordings
- Short explainer videos: "What is the AI Claims Workspace?" (60-90 seconds)
- Founder interviews and thought leadership

**Cadence:** 1-2 videos/month starting Month 3.
