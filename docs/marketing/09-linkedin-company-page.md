## About Section

*(1,847 characters — within 2,000 char limit)*

Claims professionals process 10–15 documents per claim. They toggle between 12 tabs, three systems, and five AI tools that don't talk to each other. The result isn't a workflow — it's a workaround.

Charterwell is the AI-native claims intelligence workspace for P&C insurance. We built a single environment where every document in a claim — police reports, medical records, policy declarations, compliance filings, adjuster notes — is read, understood, and connected.

Not extracted. Understood.

The difference matters. Extraction gives you data in fields. Intelligence gives you answers: Does this claim fall within coverage? Which adjuster should handle it? Does the filing meet state-specific compliance requirements? These are the decisions that drive claims outcomes, and they all start with documents.

The insurance industry runs on documents, but it has never had a platform that treats documents as first-class intelligence. Carriers have invested in fraud detection, damage appraisal, and workflow automation — all valuable, all incomplete. The document layer underneath has been ignored. Charterwell is that layer.

We call this Claims Intelligence — the category that sits between your system of record and your business decisions. Purpose-built for P&C. Understands 50+ document types. Deploys in 60–90 days. Works alongside Guidewire, Duck Creek, and every point solution you already run.

Built for VP Claims, CTO, and Claims Operations leaders at carriers who are done stitching together tools that were never designed to work together.

Your documents already contain the answers. Charterwell reads them.
