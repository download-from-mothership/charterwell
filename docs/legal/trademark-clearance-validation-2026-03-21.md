## Validation Results by Conflict

### Conflict 1: Chartwell Insurance Services / EPIC — HIGH (Confirmed)

**Validated findings:**

- chartwellins.com redirects to epicbrokers.com (confirmed)
- Chartwell operates as "one EPIC company" within EPIC's private client practice (Chicago)

- **No standalone "CHARTWELL" trademark filing by EPIC was identified** — 13 months post-acquisition, no federal filing detected

The Chartwell sub-brand appears to be diminishing within the EPIC portfolio, not expanding. This is a modestly favorable trend but does not change the risk rating — EPIC retains the resources and standing to oppose at any time.

**Risk rating: HIGH — unchanged.**

### Conflict 3: ChartWellAI — MODERATE-HIGH (Confirmed, Registered)

**Validated findings:**
- **CHARTWELL AI** (Serial No. 99558440) — **Registered**, Class 42
- **CHARTWELLAI** (Serial No. 99558514) — **Registered**, Class 42
- Goods/services confirmed as exclusively healthcare-focused: clinical notes, EHR integration, clinician decision-making support, diagnostic workflow automation, medical education, clinician well-being tools
- Both marks have matured from pending to registered since initial assessment

**This is the most significant development in the risk landscape.** These are now citable registered marks. A Section 2(d) Office Action citing one or both registrations against a CHARTERWELL Class 42 filing is a **near-certainty**.

**Critical pre-filing action:** The Office Action response brief should be drafted **before** the USPTO filing. Key arguments:

1. **Different industry verticals** — healthcare clinical AI vs. insurance/legal document processing
2. **Different end users** — clinicians and healthcare organizations vs. insurance professionals and attorneys
3. **Narrow goods/services descriptions** — both marks have tightly scoped descriptions that do not encompass each other's markets
4. **Crowded field** — multiple coexisting CHARTWELL marks across industries dilute any single mark's zone of exclusion in Class 42

**Budget impact:** $3,000–$7,000 for Office Action response. Treat as a near-certainty, not a contingency.

**Risk rating: MODERATE-HIGH (upgraded from MODERATE) — confirmed.**

## NEW Conflict Identified

### Conflict 12: Chartwell Integrated Technologies / Chartwell IT — LOW (New)

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Entity** | Chartwell Integrated Technologies, Inc. (d/b/a "Chartwell IT") |
| **Status** | Active — founded 1998 |
| **Location** | Cary, Illinois (McHenry County) |
| **Industry** | Custom software consulting — designing, building, and implementing software solutions |
| **Domain** | chartwellit.com |
| **Mark** | "CHARTWELL IT" — common-law use since 1998 |
| **Federal Registration** | No trademark registration identified |
| **Risk Level** | **LOW** |

**Analysis:** Chartwell IT is a small software consulting firm in Illinois specializing in custom application development. While they operate in the software industry (creating a theoretical Class 42 overlap), several factors limit the risk:

1. **Different trade name** — "Chartwell IT" vs. "Charterwell" includes both the CHARTWELL/CHARTERWELL distinction and the "IT" descriptor
2. **Different business model** — custom software consulting vs. AI-powered SaaS product
3. **No trademark registration** — common-law rights only, limited to their geographic and customer footprint
4. **Small scale** — a single-office boutique consultancy, not a national brand
5. **No insurance/legal focus** — general custom software, not industry-vertical

**Recommendation:** Add to monitoring list. No action required. This entity strengthens the "crowded field" argument for CHARTWELL marks in technology.

## Key Takeaways for the Board

1. **The name CHARTERWELL is registrable and defensible.** No identical mark exists in our target classes. The risk is manageable with proper preparation.

2. **The biggest change since initial assessment:** ChartWellAI's marks have registered. An Office Action is now near-certain for Class 42. Budget accordingly ($3K–$7K) and have the response brief drafted before filing.

3. **The EPIC/Chartwell Insurance conflict is stable but not declining.** EPIC has not filed a trademark, but has not abandoned the Chartwell sub-brand either. This is a watch-and-wait situation.

4. **The Chartwell Compliance risk continues to decline.** Three years of brand dormancy under Ankura is steadily eroding common-law rights.

5. **charterwell.com appears to be available.** This is a critical asset — register immediately through a domain registrar.

6. **One new conflict identified:** Chartwell IT (Illinois software consultancy). LOW risk, but adds to the monitoring list and strengthens the crowded-field defense.

7. **Correction:** Charterwell Limited (UK) is current on all Companies House filings. No strike-off risk. UK filing strategy should account for this entity being healthy and active.
